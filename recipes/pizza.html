Pizza

Description

Recipe for 3 large pizzas, that you can eat or freeze for later

Ingredients

  • 1kg of flour
  • 500ml of lukewarm water
  • 15gr of dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings of your choice (Ham, cheese, pepperonis, peppers)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, salt and yeast in a bowl
  2. Add the water slowly, stir constantly to avoid lumps
  3. Knead the dough until smooth
  4. Put the dough in a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap
  5. Let it rest until doubled in size (~1 hour)
  6. Divide the dough into 3 small balls
  7. Cover them with a bowl or plastic wrap and let them rest for 15 min
  8. Preheat the oven at 240Â°C and oil the pizza pans
  9. Stretch the dough enough to cover the pizza pan, and put sauce in it
  10. Put it in the oven for 10/15 min
  11. Pull it out of the oven and put your toppings
  12. Put it in the oven for 10/15 min
  13. Repeat for the remaining dough
  14. Let it cool for a bit and enjoy the pizza!!
